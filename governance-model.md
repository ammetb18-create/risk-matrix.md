# AI Governance Model

## Overview
This model defines a structured approach to governing AI systems in organizations, ensuring alignment with cybersecurity, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

## Governance Structure

### 1. AI Governance Lead
Responsible for oversight, accountability, and risk evaluation of AI systems.

### 2. Cybersecurity Team
Ensures system security, data protection, and monitoring of vulnerabilities.

### 3. Compliance Officer
Ensures alignment with regulatory frameworks and internal policies.

### 4. Data Management Team
Responsible for data integrity, privacy, and lifecycle management.

---

## Governance Workflow

1. AI Use Case Proposal  
2. Risk Assessment  
3. Approval & Controls Definition  
4. Deployment  
5. Continuous Monitoring  
6. Incident Response & Review  

---

## Key Principles

- Accountability  
- Transparency  
- Security  
- Compliance  
- Continuous Monitoring  

---

## Importance

A well-defined governance model ensures that AI systems are deployed responsibly, reducing risks related to cybersecurity, regulatory exposure, and operational failures.

This structure supports secure and compliant AI adoption in organizations.
